How it works

Four layers of
credibility intelligence

Every article is processed through a proprietary 4-layer pipeline the moment it's published.

01

Outlet Registry

Every outlet is assigned a base VCI score based on editorial standards, ownership structure, and historical accuracy.

02

Signal Detection

AI scans each article for risk signals — anonymous sources, unverified claims, propaganda framing, and inflammatory language.

03

Sentiment Analysis

Framing is scored for neutrality. One-sided reporting, sensationalism and advocacy language are penalised.

04

Fact-Check Match

Claims are cross-referenced against global fact-checkers including Full Fact, Snopes, PolitiFact and 12 others.

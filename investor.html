02 — THE SOLUTION

A proprietary
credibility intelligence engine

The Veritas Credibility Index (VCI) scores every article 0–100 across four independent layers, updated in real time as news breaks.

01
Outlet Registry
Every outlet assigned a base VCI based on editorial standards, ownership and track record.
02
Signal Detection
AI scans for anonymous sources, unverified claims, propaganda framing and inflammatory language.
03
Sentiment Analysis
Framing scored for neutrality. Sensationalism, one-sided reporting and advocacy penalised.
04
Fact-Check Match
Claims cross-referenced against Full Fact, Snopes, PolitiFact and 12 other global fact-checkers.

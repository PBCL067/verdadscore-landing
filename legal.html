About Verdad Score

verdadscore.com · 5 Devonport Road, Tamboerskloof

What We Do

Verdad Score is a media credibility intelligence platform that assigns real-time credibility scores to news articles, outlets, and authors. Our scoring hierarchy combines automated signal detection, external fact-check verification, and AI-assisted analysis into a single transparent score.

Our Mission

We believe a functioning democracy depends on an informed public. Verdad Score exists to make credibility visible, measurable, and actionable for everyone from individual readers to newsrooms, researchers, and institutions.

Who We Are

Operated by Pickleball Changes Lives T/A Verdad Score, 5 Devonport Road, Tamboerskloof
Cape Town, 8001, South Africa. We are an independent platform with no affiliations to any news outlet, political party, government body, or advertiser.

What We Score

  • News articles from 60+ English-language outlets across Africa, the Americas, Europe, Asia, and the Middle East
  • Outlet credibility via the Outlet Credibility Index (OCI)
  • Author credibility via the Author Credibility Index (ACI)
  • Narrative clusters — how different outlets cover the same story

What We Don't Do

  • We do not make editorial judgments about individual journalists
  • We do not accept payment from outlets in exchange for scores
  • We do not display advertising
  • We do not sell your data
